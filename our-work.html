Our Work

The jewellery industry is a very interesting field to be a part of. Not only is it exciting to experience the process of seeing a rough stone go from beginning to product, but it is also fulfilling to see the love, the beauty, the joy, and happiness that these pieces add into people’s lives on a daily basis. Diane Von Furstenberg said, “Jewellery is like the perfect spice – it compliments what’s already there.”

Our work is centred around these three categories:
Diamond Mining Our gemstones are soured from mines all over Africa, including South African and Tanzania just to mention a few.
Jewellery design and production We cut, polish, and appraise our various stones.
Sale of rough cuts, loose and finished gemstones Customers have an option to purchase our inhouse designs or come up with their own designs.
