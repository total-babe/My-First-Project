ABOUT US

Our Organisation

Solitaire stones and jewellery is 100% female owned organisation that offers a variety of tanzanite, tourmaline, emerald, smokey quartz, sapphire & an array of gemstone collectibles that will hypnotise even the most avid collector.

We are a gemstone outlet that specializes in amazing workmanship and on-time delivery of top-quality gems. We are a prominent manufacturer and international supplier of different sorts of polished and carved stones. All our stones come in a variety of cuts, shapes, and sizes. Our clients get the luxury of being part of the design of the jewellery they desire to make for their loved ones. In the field of gemstone cutting, this new art form is yielding some quite novel ideas.

As a professionally recognised organisation in the field, we are known as gemstone experts who excel in fancy cut and customized gemstones. We guarantee that when you do business with us, you will receive the highest quality diamonds at the most competitive pricing.

Company background

The start of company was inspired my father. My father was inspired by my grandfather who used to work at a mine, so he would occasionally take my father along with him to work. That is how he developed his interest in collecting stones. Growing up in a family that was big on collecting gems, I fell in love with the industry myself. When a fatal accident claimed the lives of my parents, I felt a burning passion inside me to carry on the legacy, so I took over from my father and decided to formally start this business.

Vision and Mission

Our mission is to brighten the world with our excellent jewellery that has been diligently produced. To elicit feelings of joy and pleasure, while enlightening and brightening the lives of others. Whether it's a tiny pendant or a massive diamond ring, we strive to design jewellery that inspires and keeps good memories alive.

Our vision is to be the first place that comes to mind when you're looking for unique jewellery that pays homage to traditional handmade pieces. Bring a portfolio of superbly designed jewellery brands to the globe that anticipate and fulfil people's needs and wishes. We strive to create long-term value for customers and suppliers while also contributing to a more sustainable world.

  2. Adventure: Change is exciting to us, and we aim to be innovative.
